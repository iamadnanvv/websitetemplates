About Dr. Sarah Jenkins

Dr. Jenkins is a dedicated cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of cardiovascular conditions. She believes in a holistic approach to heart health, emphasizing preventive care alongside advanced medical interventions.

Graduating top of her class from Johns Hopkins University, she completed her residency at the Mayo Clinic, focusing on interventional cardiology. Her commitment is to provide clear communication, ensuring every patient understands their condition and treatment options fully.

Comprehensive Cardiac Care

Providing state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic services tailored to your unique cardiovascular needs.

General Cardiology

Comprehensive evaluation and management of conditions like hypertension, high cholesterol, and general heart health.

Diagnostic Testing

Advanced imaging including Echocardiograms, Stress Tests, Holter Monitoring, and EKGs for precise diagnosis.

Preventive Care

Personalized risk assessments and lifestyle counseling to prevent the onset of cardiovascular disease.

Heart Failure Management

Ongoing care, medication management, and lifestyle strategies for patients living with congestive heart failure.

Arrhythmia Care

Diagnosis and treatment of irregular heartbeats, including atrial fibrillation, using the latest medical therapies.

Second Opinions

Thorough review of your medical records and prior test results to provide clarity and alternative treatment options.
