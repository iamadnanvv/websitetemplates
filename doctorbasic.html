About Dr. Sarah Jenkins

Dr. Jenkins is a licensed clinical psychologist with over 15 years of experience helping adults and couples navigate anxiety, depression, life transitions, and relationship stress. Her work integrates cognitive-behavioural therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based approaches, and trauma-informed care.

She earned her PhD in clinical psychology and completed her internship and postdoctoral fellowship at leading university medical centres. She is committed to collaborative, culturally responsive care—meeting you where you are and moving at a pace that feels safe.

Therapy & support

Evidence-based approaches tailored to your goals—whether you need short-term skills or longer-term depth work.

Individual therapy

One-to-one sessions for anxiety, depression, burnout, self-esteem, grief, and major life changes.

CBT & mindfulness

Structured tools to shift unhelpful thought patterns, regulate emotions, and build sustainable coping skills.

Trauma-informed care

Couples & relationships

Telehealth
