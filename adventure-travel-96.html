Explore Himalayan Activities

Peak Climbing

Scale majestic Himalayan peaks with our experienced mountaineering sherpas. Suitable for ambitious trekkers looking for the next challenge.

Cultural Tours

Immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Nepal. Visit ancient temples, monasteries, and world heritage sites in the Kathmandu Valley.

Heli Tours

Experience the grandeur of the Himalayas from above. Perfect for those with limited time who still want to see Everest Base Camp.

Jungle Safari

Explore Chitwan or Bardia National Parks. Spot rhinos, tigers, and diverse birdlife in Nepal's lush southern plains.

Vehicle Rental

Reliable 4WD jeeps and comfortable tourist buses for overland travel across Nepal's diverse and challenging terrain.

River Rafting

Navigate the thrilling whitewater rivers flowing down from the Himalayas. Day trips and multi-day expeditions available.
